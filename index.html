[ How we work ]

Five things we do.
Nothing else.

  • The way in

    Not a deck. Two questions: what is your big and inspiring idea — and why are you the right person to build it?

  • The read

    Every member of the team reads your writing. Everyone — before you ever walk in.

  • The decision

    One yes, from the whole firm. We invest with deep belief, or not at all.

  • The speed

    Up to $10M in 10 days, so you can get to work. Confidentiality guaranteed.

  • The room

    We show up to meetings prepared as one team. Everyone has done the reading.
